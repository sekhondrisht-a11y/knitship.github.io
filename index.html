Knitship

When you are cold and you don't have a hat, Knitship is here to help. From hats, scarves, and even head warmers we've got you covered. Knitship is a student led business that gives back to the community. We sell not for profit but warmth for the needy because we believe that every person deserves a hat. So to support our mission, we have a donation program where for every three hats sold one would be donated to charity. Every hat is hand knitted with love and care to ensure you get the best quality product. We only sell in the winter months so be sure to get your orders in early and stay warm this winter!

P.S. We do not take online or custom orders
